Arnold Zhou

Arnold is a freelance photographer based in Michigan who primarily does portrait and event photography. He currently works for the Michigan Daily, the University of Michigan's school newspaper, shooting sports, talks, and other events like TEDxUofM.

Arnold's photography centers around story-telling, framing images to express an idea. When working with clients, Arnold understands the importance of listening to their needs to create a product that aligns with their vision.
